Projects

Synthesis and Characterization of Nanoparticles for Drug Delivery for Cancer Therapy.

Synthesized and characterized CuO, SA, and hybrid Cu-SA nanoparticles for drug delivery for cancer therapy. Investigated drug-loading efficiency using ferrocene for each variants of nanoparticles.

  • Keywords: Nanoparticles synthesis and Materials testing instruments (FTIR, XRD, ZETA POTENTIAL, DLS, FESEM)
    • Guided by :- Prof. Ashok M. Raichur

    FSI- Blood Flow simulation in Clogged artery

    The computational model successfully helps in understanding how blood flow patterns are altered due to a reduced flow area caused by plaque formation, which can lead to atherosclerosis. This approach provides insights into the velocity profile, pressure distribution on the vessel wall, wall shear stress, and wall deformation under such flow conditions.

  • Keywords: Ansys Fluent, Ansys Mechanical
    •  Guided by :- Prof. Abhiram Hens

    Process Modelling of Synthesis of Methanol from Syngas

    The study demonstrates how methanol production plant can increase production rates through process modeling and simulation by optimizing separation units, recycle streams, and equilibrium-based design.
